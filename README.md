# 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Analysis of 10,000 bank customers to identify why customers 
leave and how the bank can improve retention.

---

## ❓ Business Question
Which customers are most likely to leave the bank,
and what factors drive their decision to churn?

---

## 🗂️ Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle — Bank Customer Churn
- **Size:** 10,000 customers
- **Features:** Geography, Gender, Age, Balance, 
 Products, Credit Score, Salary

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

---

## 🔍 Analysis Covered

### 1. Overall Churn Rate
- **79.6%** stayed
- **20.4%** churned

### 2. Churn by Country
| Country | Churn Rate |
|---------|-----------|
| Germany | 32.4% 🔴 |
| Spain | 16.7% ✅ |
| France | 16.2% ✅ |

### 3. Churn by Gender
| Gender | Churn Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Female | 25.1% 🔴 |
| Male | 16.5% ✅ |

### 4. Key Risk Factors
| Factor | Churn Rate |
|--------|-----------|
| Age 40-60 | 45.0% 🔴 |
| Non-Active Members | 26.9% 🔴 |
| Germany Customers | 32.4% 🔴 |
| Female Customers | 25.1% 🔴 |

---

## 💡 Key Recommendations
1. **Focus on Germany** — churn rate is double France & Spain
2. **Re-engage non-active members** — 26.9% churn rate
3. **Target Age 40-60** — highest risk segment at 45%
